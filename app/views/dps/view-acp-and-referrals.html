Rolling out the referral service

This is a new service. While we're rolling it out, referrers can only use it to
refer people to Accredited Programmes at a few prisons. At the moment, you can refer to:

  • Drake Hall (HMP & YOI)
  • Fosse Way (HMP & YOI)
  • Long Lartin (HMP)
  • Onley (HMP)
  • Peterborough Female (HMP & YOI)
  • Peterborough (HMP & YOI)
  • Stocken (HMP)
  • Whatton (HMP)

You can still refer to other prisons using your usual process.
