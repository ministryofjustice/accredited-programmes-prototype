Healthy Identity Intervention (HII)

Extremism

HII is a one-to-one programme that targets the social and psychological drivers of extremist offending. It seeks to facilitate desistance from future offending, and disengagement from an extremist group, cause and / or ideology. HII is not ideologically focused or intended to re-educate participants in a particular set of beliefs or doctrine, instead focusing on helping participants reconnect with their own personal values and beliefs, rather than being influenced by those shared by an extremist group or cause.
